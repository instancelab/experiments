What is ReExhibit?

ReExhibit is an ecosystem that allows people to collect digital items as memorabilia, and build a personal museum collection of memories.

Create a combination of VisualCache and Artifacts!

  • VisualCache: Players collect a unique memorabilia item by scanning a VisualCache code. You can use it online, or in real life for a fun scavenger hunt!
  • Artifacts: Players purchase unique memorabilia items on ReExhibit, using in-game currency, or real money. These items are unique, and can be displayed in their personal museum collection of memories.

Players love your creations, and have fond memories they want to remember. Give them a chance to collect memorabilia of their favorite moments, and build their own personal museum collection of memories.

Use VisualCache for free to engage with your fans, and use Artifacts as a new monetization stream!

Q&A

How is it different from other collectible platforms and NFTs?

"It's designed to make someone happy, not rich" - Larian's @Cromwelp regarding Scalpers driving up the price of Baldur's Gate 3 Collectors Edition

Most collectibles on the market (whether it’s trading cards, sneakers, or NFTs) attract profit-seekers who seek to scalp and flip collectibles for a quick profit, and ruin the fun for enthusiasts who just wanted to own cool memorabilia of something that they love!

Memorabilia on ReExhibit is focused on collectors, and will be kept free of: scalping, flipping, and creating awful images of bored monkeys for a quick buck.

Instead of images of random bored monkeys, Memorabilia on ReExhibit are about experiences and memories, which are used to put together your own special collection, not for making a quick buck.

How are memorabilia different from game achievements?

What is your favorite game achievement? They’re more often associated with the feeling of grinding, or following a tutorial to ‘hunt’ an achievement. The achievement comes first, and your experience comes second.

Memorabilia are all about your experience, and your memories. A fun event happens first, and then you get memorabilia to commemorate it. When you look at the memorabilia, you are fondly reminded of a sweet memory you cherish.

Games already give you cool memorable items. How’s this different?

Over the years, you’ve probably played many different games, and collected a lot of cool cosmetics and trinkets in each game. But now that you don’t play those games, you almost forget those unique items existed until you jog your memory of a specific game and a specific item.

Imagine you had a display shelf of these items! An inventory that spans across games? You can decorate your own trophy case of memories on ReExhibit.
